### Logo Design Proposal
The logo is text-based ("imediacorp.com") with the Feather of Ma'at (styled as a quill for Rhapsode synergy) placed vertically over the dot of the 'i' in "media," bending slightly right for dynamism. It's B&W for versatility, with color variants in navy/gold. Based on your selections, I've finalized options in Inter Bold (precise, digital feel) and Futura Medium (geometric, timeless). Proportional scaling ensures legibility (feather height ~1.5x text height).


**Hover Animation**: On web use, the feather subtly "floats" upward (0.5s ease-in-out CSS transition) with a gentle rotation (5 degrees), evoking balance and innovation without distraction. This enhances interactivity on the nav bar.


#### 1. Inter Bold Variant
- **Rationale**: Inter Bold's open forms and screen-optimized design convey precision, aligning with CHADD/HDPD's cross-sector diagnostics. The bold weight grounds the text, letting the feather add ethereal balance.
- **Specs**: All lowercase for modernity; kerning adjusted for flow; feather in solid black with white vein lines for contrast.
























#### 2. Futura Medium Variant
- **Rationale**: Futura Medium's geometric purity evokes timeless innovation, complementing Rhapsode's narrative tools. It provides a structured base, with the feather introducing organic movement.
- **Specs**: Mixed case optional for emphasis; even stroke weights; feather with subtle shading for depth in color versions.

Features: Responsive (mobile-first), SEO-optimized, OAuth auth, and SiteLint integration for audits (using your key for real-user insights on accessibility/performance).
